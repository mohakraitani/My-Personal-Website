About Me

Mohak completed his B.com (Hons) from the University of Delhi and then pursued his Master’s in Commerce. He qualified JRF in 2022 with 99.91% and AIR 62 in Commerce.

Before joining UQIDAR, he worked as an associate at Guardian Life Insurance. He has participated in a workshop organized by the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka in association with Emerald Publishing and Gulf Medical University. He has also presented a research paper titled “NEP 2020: Internationalization of Higher Education” at an International Conference held by BHU and attended FDP sponsored by ICSSR. His research interest includes Climate Finance & ESG Reporting.
